Controls & Objective
×

Objective

Reach the magenta goal stripe at the right edge of each level. Five levels in total. Coins give you points and some protection.

Controls

  • Move / Jump / Duck: Arrow keys / WASD, as you prefer (Space also jumps; tap again mid-air for a double jump)
  • Pause: P, or click the Pause button
  • Move: drag your thumb on the D-pad in the bottom-left
  • Jump: tap anywhere outside the D-pad (tap again mid-air for a double jump)
  • Pause: tap the Pause button
  • Best played in landscape.

Watch for

  • Green flash: a cell is being read — about to disappear
  • Red flash: a free cell is being written — about to become solid (standing in it = crushed)
  • Over time, the back of the disk fills in. Don't dawdle and don't backtrack too far.
