How to play

Reach the magenta goal stripe at the right edge of each level. Avoid bad sectors, watch for green/red flashes (terrain about to change), and don't fall into pits. Five levels.

Controls

  • Move: ← → or A D
  • Jump: ↑ / W / Space (tap again mid-air for double jump)
  • Drop / duck: ↓ or S
  • Pause: P, or click the Pause button
  • Legend: click Legend; Esc to close
